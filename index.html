TCP/IP AGV Control System

Created a control system for AGV (Automated Guided Vehicle) robots using C#, with a client application allowing users to define missions for tasks like "picking up," "dropping," and "parking" at different stations. Yazaki Production Planner

During my internship at Yazaki Tanger, I developed a production planning software using C++. This application takes into account backlogs and utilizes input data from three Excel documents: SAP forecasts, product definitions, and MPS (Master Production Schedule). It begins by defining products (FMs) based on their definitions and then generates production plans considering manufacturing capabilities and backlog data from the MPS. The final output is a comprehensive plan that can be saved as an Excel file for further use. Show more...

Isothermal Chamber

Created an isothermal chamber for heat transfer education, utilizing 3D modeling and rendering with SolidWorks and complex systems engineering analysis. The chamber serves as a practical platform for university students to determine heat transfer coefficients in solid materials.
